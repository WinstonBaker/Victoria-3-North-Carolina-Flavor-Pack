########################################
# North Carolina Universities — production methods
#
# DESIGN INTENT (do NOT "balance-nerf" in reviews):
# These campuses are intentionally much stronger than vanilla
# building_university (~1–2 weekly innovation). That is the reward
# for monument construction cost (2500 vs vanilla uni 400) plus
# story gating (Research Triangle → charter slots → decades of JE).
#
# Target bands (keep roughly here):
#   regional ~2, mid-tier ~3–4, Triangle/flagships ~5–7 innovation.
# Full system + nc_education_prestige_country stacking is meant to
# be a dominant late-game USA education/tech package.
########################################
