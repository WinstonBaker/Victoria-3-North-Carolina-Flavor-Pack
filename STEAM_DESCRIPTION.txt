[h1]North Carolina Flavor Pack[/h1]
Tar Heel flavor for Victoria 3 (1.13.*) — journal chains, decisions, unique buildings, companies, characters, and state traits, without a full USA overhaul.

This is not a strict history simulator. It borrows North Carolina history for immersion and pacing, then bends timelines so content fits Victoria 3 systems and stays fun.

[h2]Content[/h2]
[list]
[*][b]Universities[/b] — UNC System (Chapel Hill, Research Triangle, 14 regional charters), Wake Forest relocation, education prestige
[*][b]Outer Banks[/b] — Lighthouse program, fuel PMs, storm/wreck pulses, coastal reconstruction
[*][b]Rail[/b] — Wilmington & Weldon → Atlantic & NC → Western NC → Carolina Rail Network
[*][b]Piedmont industry[/b] — Textile / power / furniture / brand companies, mill village, naval stores
[*][b]Tobacco[/b] — Brightleaf companies (Duke / Reynolds / Liggett)
[*][b]Coast & century[/b] — Cape Fear / Morehead ports, Wright brothers, Reconstruction → New South
[*][b]Characters[/b] — Historical industrialists & builders (Duke, Reynolds, Bradham, Vanderbilt, Wrights, …)
[*][b]Landmarks[/b] — State Capitol, Biltmore, state traits, Carolina gold belt
[/list]

[h2]Notes[/h2]
[list]
[*]Play as the USA (or any country that owns incorporated North Carolina) for the full journal tab.
[*]Compatible with vanilla USA; no hard dependencies on other flavor mods.
[*]Company [b]prestige goods[/b] require the Charters of Commerce DLC.
[*]Character portraits are placeholders (no custom DNA yet).
[*]Research Triangle unlocks after Tar Heel Legacy (pacing gate, not a calendar lock).
[/list]
